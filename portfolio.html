AIESEC Membership Experience



In B2C, AIESEC'S customers are youth aged between 18 and 30. As a B2C member, I have the opportunity to take a look at the stages of the customer flow that consumers go through and interact heavily with a global exchange management platform: experience.aiesec.org (EXPA). One of the most significant skills that is provided by AIESEC is to understand the marketing messages needed to properly attract the right customer profiles. When you have the customer's attention, it's important to nurture the customer and follow up with them closely during the consideration phase. You have to work towards closing and securing the customer's sale.On the other hand, I was able to improve my communication and presentation skills, take initiatives during my tenure and enhance my soft skills. Moreover, I have the chance to realize the importance of keeping a positive attitude and try my best to motivate the people around me. However, getting to the approve and closing the sale is half the battle because as B2C I should deliver my promise to my customer the Exchange Participant.Subsequently, after the Exchange Participants have completed their exchange experience, I learned how to re-integrate them back into the organization. Turn them into a brand advocate and develop a life-long connection.
